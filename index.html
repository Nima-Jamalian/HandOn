Idea 💡

My research mainly focuses on hand tracking 🖐️ in Virtual reality. I am trying to understand how hand tracking in VR affects the user and understand the differences when using controller 🎮 vs. ✋ hand tracking. My experiment focuses on object selection, object manipulation.

How it work!

  • You book a time to come to our VR lab and participate in the experiment. You don't have to bring anything, we will provide you with the VR equipment.
  • The experiment takes around only 10 minutes so it won’t take much of your time ⌚.
  • The experiment is actually a game 🎮, once you are here I explain to you how it works and then you jump into VR.
  • There will be a questionnaire which also take place in VR, in here you answer series of question related to experience you had.
  • I will not collect any personal information from you, the data collected is fully anonymised. 👻
  • The experiment has two version hand tracking and controller mode you play through both.
